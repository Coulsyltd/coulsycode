# Competitor Analysis: York Web Development Agencies

**Analysis Date:** January 2026  
**Competitors:** Castlegate IT, See Green, Andersen Lab

## Key Findings: What We're Missing

### 1. **Client Testimonials & Social Proof** ⭐ CRITICAL
**What they have:**
- Castlegate IT: Multiple testimonials with names, companies, and photos
- See Green: "What our clients say" section with 60+ reviews
- Both display client logos prominently

**What we need:**
- Add testimonials section to homepage
- Create dedicated testimonials/reviews page
- Display client logos (even if anonymized initially)
- Add trust badges/certifications

### 2. **Detailed Case Studies** ⭐ CRITICAL
**What they have:**
- Castlegate IT: Industry-specific case studies (manufacturing, charities, property, etc.)
- See Green: Detailed portfolio with project descriptions
- Both show "before/after" or project highlights

**What we need:**
- Expand portfolio with detailed case studies
- Add industry-specific examples
- Include results/metrics (e.g., "Increased leads by 40%")
- Show project process/workflow

### 3. **Industry-Specific Solutions** ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- Castlegate IT: Dedicated pages for manufacturing, charities, academic, property, e-commerce
- See Green: Sector-specific examples in portfolio
- Both show expertise in specific industries

**What we need:**
- Create industry-specific solution pages:
  - Manufacturing & Engineering
  - Charities & Not-for-Profit
  - Property & Construction
  - E-commerce & Retail
  - Professional Services
- Each page should have relevant case studies

### 4. **Local York/Yorkshire Positioning** ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- See Green: "A proud Yorkshire-based agency" prominently displayed
- Castlegate IT: "Based in York" mentioned
- Both emphasize local presence

**What we need:**
- Add "York-based" or "Yorkshire" messaging to homepage
- Create "Why York" or local benefits section
- Mention local clients/case studies
- Add local landmarks or references

### 5. **Certifications & Trust Signals** ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- See Green: Good Business Charter, Cyber Essentials, Nominet Member
- Castlegate IT: Professional accreditations
- Both show partner logos

**What we need:**
- Add certifications section (if applicable)
- Display technology partner logos
- Add security/privacy badges
- Show industry memberships

### 6. **Blog/News Section** ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- See Green: Blog with latest posts (AI vs Web Builders, website tips)
- Castlegate IT: Blog section
- Both show thought leadership

**What we need:**
- Create blog section
- Add latest posts to homepage
- Write about web development trends
- SEO benefits from fresh content

### 7. **Year in Review / Company Highlights** ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- Castlegate IT: "2024 year in review" section
- Shows company growth and achievements

**What we need:**
- Add company milestones section
- Show project count or years of experience
- Highlight achievements/awards

### 8. **More Detailed Service Pages** ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- Castlegate IT: Detailed service pages (WordPress, hosting, Google Ads, SEO)
- See Green: Service pages with sub-services
- Both explain process and benefits

**What we need:**
- Expand service pages with more detail
- Add sub-services (e.g., WordPress development under websites)
- Include pricing guidance or "starting from" info
- Show technology stack for each service

### 9. **Client Logo Wall** ⭐ MEDIUM PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- Both display client logos prominently
- Shows credibility and social proof

**What we need:**
- Add "Who we work with" section
- Display client logos (with permission)
- Can start with anonymized versions

### 10. **Sustainability/Ethics Messaging** ⭐ LOW PRIORITY
**What they have:**
- See Green: Carbon neutral, sustainability focus
- Appeals to environmentally conscious clients

**What we need:**
- Consider adding sustainability messaging
- Mention eco-friendly hosting options
- Green web development practices
